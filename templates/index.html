You can also create new message templates! To do so, simply type a message as you would normally and in any place where you want to insert information unique to each guest or company, write that in all capital letters and seperate it from other parts of the sentence with either punctuation or spaces, as shown below.

  • "FIRSTNAME" = first name of guest
  • "LASTNAME" = last name of guest
  • "COMPANYNAME = name of company"
  • "ROOMNUMBER" = the room number for a particular guest's reservation
  • "CITY" = the city the company is located in
  • "TIMEZONE" = the timezone the company is located in

Example: "Hello FIRSTNAME LASTNAME! Room ROOMNUMBER is ready for you. We hope you enjoy your stay at CITY COMPANYNAME."
